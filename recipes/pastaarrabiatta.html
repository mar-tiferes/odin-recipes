Pasta Arrabiatta Recipe

Best Arrabiatta Recipe ever!!

This is the best Arrabiatta recipe you'll find on the internet!
Nothing compares to it. I'll show you how, in a few simple steps
you'll be able to make it. And no more than 5 (yes 5!) ingredients!!
IBET you'll love it!!!

Ingredients

  • 500g Penne Pasta
  • bunch of Parsley
  • 2 tinned tomatoes
  • 1kg chilli flakes (watch your stomach)
  • 250g grated cheese

Step by step

  1. The first step is to cook the sauce with the tinned tomatoes. just chuck them on the
    microwave oven and set them to cook for 2 minutes at 700W
  2. Next, once the sauce is ready, cook the pasta al dente
  3. Chuck the pasta to the pan you were cooking the sauce and toss them arround until everything is coated
  4. Finish with a layer of sauce and the grated cheese on top
  5. Place in the oven at 1400 C and burn it, but burn it well.
