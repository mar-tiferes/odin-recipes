Lasagna Recipe

Best Lasagna recipe (so easy!)

This is the best lasagna recipe you'll find on the internet!
Nothing compares to it. I'll show you how, in a few simple steps
you'll be able to make it. And no more than 5 (yes 5!) ingredients!!
How does THAT sound?!

Ingredients

  • 16 Lasagna sheets
  • 2kg 15% mince beef
  • 2 tinned tomatoes
  • 1 jar white sauce
  • 250g grated cheese

Step by step

  1. The first step is to cook the sauce with the tinned tomatoes. just chuck them on the
    microwave oven and set them to cook for 2 minutes at 700W
  2. Next, once the sauce is ready, swipe a dollop of the sauce on the oven-safe plate
    and line up the lasagna sheets snuggly
  3. Place the cooked minced beef on top of the sheets and cover with another layer of Lasagna sheets
  4. Carry on doing this until you finish both the beef and the sheets
  5. Finish with a layer of sauce and the grated cheese on top
  6. Place in the oven at 1400 C and burn it, but burn it well.
