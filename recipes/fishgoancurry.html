Ingredients

  • 500g Fish caught in GOA
  • bunch of Parsley
  • 2 tinned tomatoes
  • 1kg chilli flakes (watch your stomach and ass by now)
  • 2 Coconut trees

Step by step

  1. The first step is to cook the sauce with the tinned tomatoes. just chuck them on the
    microwave oven and set them to cook for 2 minutes at 700W
  2. Next, once the sauce is ready, shake the trees until the cocounts fall to the ground (watch your head)
  3. Chuck the milky filling onto the pan with the tomato sauce
  4. Place the fish in this mixture and let it cook for 10 minutes
  5. Place in the oven at 1400 C and burn it, but burn it well. BTW I love burnt stuff (you might've realised by now)
